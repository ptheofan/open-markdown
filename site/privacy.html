Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2025

Overview

Markdown Viewer is a local-only desktop application. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app to respect it completely.

Data Collection

Markdown Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No network requests are made by the application
  • No user accounts or registration

Local Processing

All Markdown files are read and rendered entirely on your device. No file contents are ever sent to any server or third party. The app operates fully offline.

Third-Party Services

Markdown Viewer does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

Because Markdown Viewer does not collect any data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
